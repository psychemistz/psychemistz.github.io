Siemens Healthineers is a globally renowned medical technology company. It would be a wonderful opportunity to work with an experienced team of researchers. \lettersection{Why Me?}
I have over 5 years of experience in machine learning and medical imaging. I have worked on a number of projects related to Medical Image reconstruction, denoising, and super-resolution. During my studies I managed to produce excellent works that are already published in reputable conferences/journals including MICCAI, ISBI, IEEE-TMI, IEEE-TUFFC, IEEE/ACM-TCBB and IEEE-TNNLS, etc. I am an active participant of MICCAI, IUS, ISBI and SPIE conferences and have presented a number of ORAL and POSTER papers. I am self-motivated and a passionate learner and based on my academic performance in 2019 I was awarded with BISPL's best researcher award. I believe my profile best fits the aforementioned position.
